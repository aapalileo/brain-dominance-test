PP Telegraf web fonts go here.

Add these files (from your Pangram Pangram / Bootleg license) to this folder:
  PPTelegraf-Regular.woff2   (weight 400)
  PPTelegraf-Medium.woff2    (weight 500)
  PPTelegraf-Semibold.woff2  (weight 600)
  PPTelegraf-Bold.woff2      (weight 700)

The @font-face rules in styles.css already point here. If your filenames or
weights differ, update the src/font-weight lines at the top of styles.css to match.
Until the files are added, the site falls back to Space Grotesk automatically.
